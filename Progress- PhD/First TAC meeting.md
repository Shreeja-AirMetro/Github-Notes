The **first TAC meeting** is not an exam but an opportunity for you to receive feedback, confirm your project’s feasibility, and discuss your plans. At one year into your PhD, you are expected to present the following:
- **Research motivation and context:** Why is your topic important? What is the current state of the field?
- **Main research questions or hypotheses:** What are you aiming to discover or prove?
- **Project goals and objectives:** What do you hope to achieve with your PhD project
- ==It is **not necessary to already present results** at this stage; focus on your approach, progress, and plans==
-  **Proposed methodology:** Explain your planned methods and why you chose them.
- **Timeline:** Present a realistic plan for the coming year, including key milestones and deliverables.
- **Expected outcomes:** What do you hope to accomplish in the next phase
