### Good Scientific Practice 
Module 1
to the interdisciplinary online learning programme "Good Academic Practice during Doctoral Studies"!  
   
In this learning programme, we will explain what you should consider in order to be successful with your doctoral studies.   

![](https://bildungsportal.sachsen.de/scormruntime/course-packages/HOT_OPAL-461742080031726046214171/pages/P1/image.bee7623d596d492aa630b81f500021ce.png?1762186768279)

Four modules are available to you after the chapter on getting started:

- **Background**  
Here you will learn about what characterizes good academic practice and what kinds of misconduct there are.   

- **Orientation Phase**  
This chapter explains how you can successfully start your doctoral studies and which legal aspects  
are relevant to you.  

- **Working Phase**  
You will receive important information regarding how to organize your literature and research, how to correctly cite text and images and how to save your results.

**Completion Phase**  
The last chapter describes at what point you are considered to be an author of a publication and how the publication process works.  
  
This first module "**Getting Started**" 
- offers you an overview of the current learning programme
- helps you to understand the navigation and the learning objective
For completing the following modules, you will need approximately 15 minutes per module.
